About Us

          GroupOrange intend to cater to entire society for women hygiene Issues. The women Hygiene and sanitation is one of the prime object of our Nation with a Government of India, National Commission for Women’s and Government Of Maharashtra under “ Swatch Bharat Abhiyaan ”. The Objective of these proposal is to contribute to promote the safe & hygienic Sanitary Practices among women’s/Girls at the place they spend their maximum time in Schools ,College,Offices, Market, Hostels, Bus Stops, Railway Stations and Industries too. Orange Instruments & Engineering, Nagpur is the Organization to provide round the Clock a good quality “Sanitary Napkin Automated /Manual Vending Machine” and “Sanitary Napkin Burning Machine” and also having Sanitary Pads of different sizes.
